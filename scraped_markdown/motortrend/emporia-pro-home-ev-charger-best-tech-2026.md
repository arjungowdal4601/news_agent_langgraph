__Ford Mustang Mach-E__But the Emporia Pro isn’t a 2026 *MotorTrend *Best Tech winner just because it’s a better extension cord. It earns the accolade based on its ability to be more than that. When you install its included Vue Energy Monitor, the Emporia Pro becomes smart enough to potentially save you thousands of dollars.

Qmerit, a national installer of home EV chargers, says that 4.5 percent of its quotes require an electrical panel upgrade. That might sound like a small number, but if you’re one of the unlucky few in that position, the implications can be huge. A panel replacement costs about $4,100 on average, according to Qmerit.

Emporia can help you eliminate that expense altogether. Slightly larger than your phone, the Vue Energy Monitor wires into your electrical panel and measures the current flowing through the main service cables. The Emporia Pro uses that information to reduce its power output as necessary, so your EV never trips the main breaker when it’s charging. It’s a brilliant, cost-effective solution to a monstrously expensive problem for EV drivers with older homes. In homes with solar panels, the Emporia Pro and Vue Energy Monitor can also be configured so your EV only charges using free and clean energy when your panels are producing more electricity than your house is consuming.
