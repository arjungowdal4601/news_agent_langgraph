Reviewed by [Alex Leanse](https://www.motortrend.com/staff/alex-leanse)

## New Sierra Engines

In the 2027 Sierra 1500, some powertrain components might carry over from the current generation, and others will be new and improved.

A highlight would be the arrival of new small-block V-8 engines. Output should exceed the 355 hp and 383 lb-ft made by the current 5.3-liter V-8, and 420 hp and 460 lb-ft from the 6.2-liter V-8. Fuel economy could get a little better, too. Expect a 10-speed automatic transmission to continue behind any V-8 engine.

The 2.7-liter turbocharged I-4 might carry over, still making 310 hp and 430 lb-ft of torque through an eight-speed automatic. Likewise, the Duramax 3.0-liter turbodiesel I-6 could return with 305 hp and 495 lb-ft.

## Is the Sierra 1500 Going Electric?

There's already an [all-electric Sierra](https://www.motortrend.com/cars/gmc/sierra-ev), but GMC could introduce an optional electrified powertrain for the conventional Sierra 1500. How that might be engineered is unknown. It could be a plug-in hybrid (PHEV) system for a V-8 engine, improving its fuel economy and performance.

Or, it could adopt an extended range (EREV) setup, where a smaller engine like the turbocharged I-4 is used as a generator for electric motors that power the wheels, rather than driving the wheels itself. Rivals [Ford and Ram](https://www.motortrend.com/news/electric-ford-f-150-lightning-replacement-extended-range-hybrid-erev) have announced EREV powertrains for their half-ton trucks.

## Safety and Driver Assist Features

Expect similar active safety and driver aids on the 2027 Sierra 1500 as the 2026 model. Those should include automatic emergency braking, rear cross-traffic alert, blind-spot monitoring with trailer coverage, lane keep assist, and adaptive cruise control on the list. [General Motors’ Super Cruise](https://www.motortrend.com/news/best-tech-2025-gm-super-cruise-driver-assistance) hands-free driving system will be optional or included depending on the truck’s spec.

## Cab and Bed Configurations

Even as the 2027 Sierra 1500 evolves, it should continue to offer customary cab and bed options. Up front will be a two-door Regular Cab, four-door Double Cab, or bigger four-door Crew Cab. Behind, buyers will be able to choose between short, medium, and long beds. The outgoing model offers 5.8-foot, 6.6-foot, and 8.0-foot lengths.

## Technology

The current Sierra 1500 already put an emphasis on tech, and the 2027 redesign is likely to increase that. Instead of the basic 7.0-inch and 13.4-inch touchscreens used in the 2026 model, the new 2027 Sierra 1500 might get a standard 16.8-inch touchscreen like what’s seen in the Sierra EV.

A standard driver display could be included, as a head-up display and digital rearview mirror stay optional. Count on many USB-C ports and a wireless charging pad. Hopefully, the tech upgrades don’t supplant conventional, user-friendly buttons and knobs for basic functions.
