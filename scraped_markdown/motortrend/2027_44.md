## 2027 Mercedes-Benz GLE-Class Expert Review

Reviewed by [Billy Rehbock](https://www.motortrend.com/staff/billy-rehbock)

One of the heavy hitters in [ the midsize luxury SUV segment](https://www.motortrend.com/rankings/suvs/luxury/midsize) returns with a major overhaul. The 2027 Mercedes-Benz GLE-Class is back in SUV and fastback coupe body styles with a thorough refresh, which touches everything from cosmetics, trim, and powertrains. Mercedes places greater emphasis on tech than ever with its MB.OS operating system and artificial intelligence. Rivals to the 2027 Mercedes-Benz GLE-Class include the BMW X5, Genesis GV80, and Lexus RX.

## What’s New

**Basics**

- Revised engines with more power
- Plug-in hybrid variant now equipped with an inline-six
- Computer control unit running MB.OS with water cooling
- E-Active Body Control suspension retuned with a firmer, sportier setup
- Overhauled Mercedes-AMG GLE Hybrid 4Matic+ SUV and coupe variants

**Exterior **

- A reworked front fascia with new headlights, an illuminated grille, and an illuminated badge
- Restyled rear fascia with updated taillights
- Digital Light technology projects a 40-percent larger field while consuming 50 percent less energy
- Standard panoramic sliding roof
- 10 cameras, up to five radar sensors, and 12 ultrasonic sensors

**Interior**

- Standard MBUX Superscreen with three 12.3-inch displays
- An MBUX Virtual Assistant with conversational voice commands and a customizable avatar
- “Rocker-and-roller” controls on the steering wheel
- The Energizing Air Control feature uses a new electric air filter that purifies the cabin air every 90 seconds
- New interior colors and trim such as “Beech Brown”

Mercedes-Benz introduced the current-generation GLE-Class for the 2019 model year and has kept it fresh with new variants, features, and tech ever since. Previously, we favored the mid-grade versions of the luxury midsize SUV, but the 2027 Mercedes-Benz GLE-Class’ facelift appears to be addressing prior inconsistency with major enhancements.

## Performance

After adding a RWD variant of the GLE350 to the lineup for the 2025 model year, the updated 2027 Mercedes-Benz GLE arrives with standard all-wheel drive. That makes the GLE350 4Matic the entry-level trim for the new model year. Mercedes claims enhanced performance and responsiveness for this variant’s 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine, which develops 255 hp and 295 lb-ft of torque. It now comes standard with an electric compressor. Mercedes-Benz cites a 0–60 mph time of 7.1 seconds for the GLE350 4Matic.

The 2027 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 4Matic gets a worked-over 3.0-liter inline-six engine developing 12 percent more torque than the prior year. For 2027, the straight-six produces 375 hp and 413 lb-ft of torque. That’s a healthy increase of 44 lb-ft. Mercedes-Benz cites changes such as an upgraded electric auxiliary compressor, optimized intake and exhaust ports for the cylinder heads, and a revised intake camshaft. The 0–60 mph sprint falls to 5.2 seconds.

Next in the lineup is the GLE500e 4Matic plug-in hybrid, which replaces the GLE450e. It receives an inline-six engine producing 322 hp rather than the outgoing model’s 248-hp turbo-four, netting a 74-hp increase in engine output. Mercedes-Benz claims all-electric range of 106 km according to the European WLTP cycle, which should translate to an EPA rating of around 62 miles. The GLE500e tops out at 87 mph while only using battery power. Mercedes claims a 0–60 mph dash of 5.5 seconds for the plug-in GLE-Class, a few tenths slower than the gas-only variant.

At the top of the 2027 GLE-Class range sits the GLE580 4Matic, which is powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V-8 engine now using a flat-plane crankshaft instead of a cross-plane design as before. For the new model year, output climbs from 510 hp to 530 hp and torque increases from 538 lb-ft to 553 lb-ft. Mercedes-Benz says the V-8’s responsiveness and smoothness have improved. The most powerful GLE-Class variant accelerates from 0–60 mph in a claimed 4.4 seconds.

Mercedes-AMG offers the GLE53 Hybrid 4Matic+, which is equipped with a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine developing 443 hp and 443 lb-ft of torque paired with a 181-hp electric motor. Total system output is 577 hp and 553 lb-ft of torque, which propels the GLE53 from 0–60 mph in 4.4 seconds.

The performance model is also a plug-in hybrid with a 9.6-kW onboard charger and 60-kW fast-charging capability. The battery can be charged from 10 to 80 percent in 20 minutes. The AMG variant of the GLE comes standard with AMG Ride Control+ air suspension with adaptative adjustable damping.

All versions of the GLE-Class receive a nine-speed transmission. Mercedes-Benz says the GLE-Class can tow up to 7,700 pounds, aided by trailer stabilization. The GLE-Class can correct unwanted trailer movements automatically and can fully apply the brakes if necessary.

## Safety and Driver-Assist Features

Mercedes-Benz equips the 2027 GLE-Class with 10 cameras, up to five radar sensors, and 12 ultrasonic sensors to support its driver-assist features.

### Standard Features

The 2027 Mercedes-Benz GLE-Class is equipped with the MB.Drive Standard suite of active safety technology. By default, the latest GLE gets standard parking sensors and a 360-degree camera view. There’s a rim protection warning that gives a distance alert to help you avoid curbing your wheels.

Other standard tech includes automatic emergency braking, adaptive cruise control, blind-spot monitoring, autonomous parking, automatic high-beams, rain-sensing wipers, and driver focus monitoring.

### Available Features

As is typical for Mercedes-Benz, in the 2027 GLE-Class the best driver assistance technology is optional. MB.Drive Assist and MB.Drive Assist Pro packages are available. MB. Drive Parking Assist, which scans and finds available parking spaces before automatically making a parking maneuver at speeds up to 3 mph, has been updated to be more accurate and 60 percent faster than the prior version. You can also leave a parking space with MB. Drive Parking Assist even if you didn’t use the system to park.

## Cargo Space and Interior Room

The 2027 Mercedes-Benz GLE-Class has a spacious interior for second row passengers and cargo when the rear seats are flipped down. Mercedes-Benz says a pair of seats is available for the third row, with enough legroom for occupants as tall as 5’10”.

## Technology

### Infotainment

The 2027 Mercedes-Benz GLE-Class is standard with the MBUX Superscreen, which houses three 12.3-inch displays under a single panel. Conversational voice commands are handled by the MBUX Virtual Assistant. The AI avatar is always present on the central screen with animations to signal if it’s active or waiting to be activated. Navigation is powered by Google Maps integrated into the MBUX interface. A digital key allows you to unlock your car by walking up to the vehicle with your phone in your pocket.

### Sound System

A premium sound system from Burmester is available with 15 speakers and a maximum output of 710 watts.

### Available Upgrades

Augmented reality navigation is available with three-dimensional arrows providing explicit directions and virtual overlays for additional information or warnings. An air filtration system refreshes the cabin air every 90 seconds.
