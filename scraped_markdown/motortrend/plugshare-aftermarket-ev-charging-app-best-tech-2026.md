# Best Tech 2026: The Free PlugShare App Keeps Making EV Charging Easy

There’s enough stress around EV living. PlugShare makes things better.

Despite the present tumult surrounding EVs, [ public charging is improving](https://www.motortrend.com/features/ionna-ev-charging-hq-visit-station-review), driven in part by legions of faithful owners who continue to push the boundaries of the all-electric frontier. But beyond the daily dependability an at-home plug provides, concerns around long-haul realities remain. Today’s EV road trips aren’t defined by range so much anymore; it’s more about what they find when they stop to recharge. Miss that reliable bank of chargers and arrive at a broken stall instead or discover a “public” station that’s private, and your itinerary can fray like a cut cable.

PlugShare remains the preeminent way to avoid and otherwise navigate those types of pitfalls, which is why it’s the [ repeat](https://www.motortrend.com/news/best-tech-2025-plugshare-aftermarket-ev-charging-app) winner of our 2026 Best Tech Aftermarket App award.

### What Is PlugShare?

PlugShare is a charging-finder and trip-planning app built around the idea that the most useful charging info is real-world info. PlugShare continually aggregates station information from across a number of networks then provides hugely useful data that the network apps often don't, namely recent experiences from drivers who used (or attempted to use) the chargers. Reviews, check-ins, photos, and notes create a living record of what’s working, what’s flaky, and what you should steer clear of.
