Yvonne Bettkober has left Audi and the Volkswagen Group with immediate effect at her own request and by mutual agreement, stepping down from her role leading the automaker’s transformation, consulting and organisation department. Daniel Kauer, General Secretary and Head of Corporate Strategy, will assume interim responsibility for transformation alongside his existing duties.

Bettkober joined Audi at a period of structural realignment, with her remit covering cost reduction, productivity improvement and organisational change. A permanent successor has not yet been announced.

In a statement, Gernot Döllner, Chief Executive of Audi, said: “On behalf of the entire Board of Management team, I want to thank Yvonne Bettkober for her outstanding leadership and the significant momentum she created during a defining phase for Audi. She played a vital role in making Audi more focused, efficient, and agile, while further developing a culture of responsibility and collaboration. Transformation is only possible as a team — and Team Audi will move forward and continue to shape this change together. We respect Yvonne’s decision and wish her all the best for the future.”
