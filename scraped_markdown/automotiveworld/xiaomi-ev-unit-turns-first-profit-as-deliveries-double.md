Automotive newcomer Xiaomi's smart electric vehicle (EV) and AI segment posted its first full-year operating profit of CN¥900m (US$132m) in 2025, with revenue surging 223.8% to CN¥106.1bn. The result came on deliveries of 411,082 vehicles, doubling its 2025 performance, with gross margin expanding 5.8 percentage points to 24.3%.
