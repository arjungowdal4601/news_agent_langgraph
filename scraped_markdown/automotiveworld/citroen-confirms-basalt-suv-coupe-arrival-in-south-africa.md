Citroën South Africa has confirmed the imminent arrival of the all-new Basalt, an SUV-coupé positioned in the local B-segment and scheduled to go on sale by the end of Q1 2026. The model introduces a new body style for the brand in South Africa, combining an elevated stance and coupé roofline with the practicality of a compact passenger vehicle.

![](https://media.automotiveworld.com/app/uploads/2026/03/25111930/citroen-basalt-1024x683.jpg)


The Basalt forms part of Citroën’s global C-CUBED programme, which centres on vehicles described as simple, comfortable, sustainable and daring. It sits between a traditional hatchback and an SUV in the brand’s line-up, targeting young professionals and growing families seeking a more expressive alternative to conventional compact vehicles while remaining accessible to own and operate.

Key features include elevated ground clearance suited to diverse South African road conditions, generous interior space with a rear-seat comfort focus, Citroën’s Advanced Comfort programme, and contemporary infotainment and connectivity. The Basalt complements the recently updated C3 and C3 Aircross range, further strengthening Citroën’s presence in South Africa’s high-volume passenger vehicle segments. Full specifications and pricing are to be confirmed closer to the official launch date.
