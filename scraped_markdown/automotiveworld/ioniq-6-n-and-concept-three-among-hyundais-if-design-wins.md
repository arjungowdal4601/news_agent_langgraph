Hyundai Motor has secured 21 awards at the iF Design Award 2026, with vehicle and automotive product designs among the recognised entries. The Ioniq 6 N high-performance electric sedan, the Concept Three compact electric vehicle and the MobED Urban Hopper and Golf robotic mobility platform variants all received recognition in the product design categories.

![](https://media.automotiveworld.com/app/uploads/2026/03/26124748/hyundai-ioniq-6n-1024x576.jpg)

The Ioniq 6 N win builds on the production model’s existing profile, while Concept Three represents Hyundai’s first compact electric concept under the Ioniq sub-brand, developed around what the company calls its Art of Steel design language. The MobED Urban Hopper and Golf variants extend the adaptable robotic mobility platform into urban and leisure applications.

![](https://media.automotiveworld.com/app/uploads/2026/03/26125053/hyundai-concept-three-1024x613.jpg)

Automotive-adjacent product designs also featured among the wins. The EV Frunk Cooler Bag, which repurposes the front trunk as a lifestyle storage space, and the Pixel Diffuser, a vehicle air freshener drawing on the Ioniq lineup’s pixel design identity, both received awards in the product design category.

![](https://media.automotiveworld.com/app/uploads/2026/03/26125733/hyundai-diffuser-and-ev-frunk-cooler-bag-1024x329.jpg)

The iF Design Award is an annual competition judged by an international panel. Hyundai said its 2026 tally surpassed its total from the previous year’s event.
