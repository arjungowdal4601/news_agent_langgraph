Stellantis will use the 2026 New York International Auto Show (NYIAS) to reveal the refreshed 2027 Chrysler Pacifica and the all-new 2027 Ram ProMaster City commercial van, alongside a special-edition Dodge Durango marking the United States’ 250th anniversary. The press conference, led by Matt McAlear, Chief Executive of the Chrysler and Dodge brands, takes place on 1 April 2026 at the Jacob K. Javits Convention Center.

|
|
|

The 2027 Ram ProMaster City joins the Ram Professional lineup as a compact commercial van aimed at business customers. The Durango America250 is a limited-edition production vehicle, with Stellantis’ four US brands serving as exclusive automotive sponsors of the America250 anniversary celebration.

Public show days run from 3 to 12 April 2026, with all six Stellantis brands represented. Jeep is marking its 85th anniversary with a range of models including the fully electric 2026 Jeep Recon, rated at 650 horsepower and an estimated 250 miles of range. The Ram lineup features the return of the 5.7-litre HEMI V-8 to the 1500 series, while Dodge is debuting a new exterior colour on the 550-horsepower Charger Scat Pack. Alfa Romeo is showing the refreshed 2026 Tonale and Fiat is presenting the 500e Giorgio Armani Collector’s Edition.
