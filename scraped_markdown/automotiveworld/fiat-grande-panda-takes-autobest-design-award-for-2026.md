Fiat's Grande Panda has taken the Design category at the 2026 Autobest Conquest Awards, combining European public and jury voting

Fiat’s Grande Panda has won the Design category at the 2026 Autobest Conquest Awards, a newly introduced competition in which public votes from across Europe were combined with jury scoring from 32 countries. The award, which recognises what organisers describe as the best car design in Europe, was announced during a live broadcast on 28 March.

![](https://media.automotiveworld.com/app/uploads/2026/03/31132351/fiat-grande-panda-2026-autobest-conquest-award-1024x683.jpg)


The Conquest competition, launched for the first time this year, invited the public to select vehicles across three categories: Design, Technology and User-Friendliness. Grande Panda secured the Design title ahead of five other finalists.

The award follows a period of broader recognition for the model, which has been positioned by Fiat as a core offering in the urban mobility segment.
