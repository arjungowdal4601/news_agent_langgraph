Dodge has introduced the 2026 Durango R/T 392 Launch Edition, making the 6.4-litre HEMI V8 engine standard on the R/T model for the first time. The SUV produces 475 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque, which Dodge says makes it the most powerful three-row SUV available under US$50,000, with orders now open across all 50 states from US$49,995.

Compared with the 2025 Durango R/T, the new model delivers 32% more horsepower and 21% more torque, with the 0-60 mph time dropping from 6.2 to 4.4 seconds. An NHRA-certified quarter-mile time of 12.9 seconds is quoted alongside a top speed of 160 mph.

![](https://media.automotiveworld.com/app/uploads/2026/03/25143448/dodge-durango-r-t-hemi-power-1024x683.jpg)


Power is transmitted via an eight-speed TorqueFlite automatic with standard all-wheel drive. Standard equipment includes adaptive damping suspension, Brembo six-piston front and four-piston rear brakes, an electronic limited-slip rear axle, and high-performance exhaust. Towing capacity is rated at 8,700 lb.

“The new Durango R/T 392 Launch Edition instantly sets the value-performance benchmark for three-row SUVs,” Matt McAlear, Dodge Chief Executive, said. “As the only muscle SUV of its kind — with a heart-pounding 4.4-second 0-60 and an NHRA-certified 12.9-second quarter-mile — the Durango R/T 392 brings authentic Dodge muscle to more drivers than ever.”

A Premium variant, adding a Harman Kardon audio system, carbon-fibre interior accents and upgraded rotors, starts at US$57,595.
