Honda's decision to [terminate](https://www.automotiveworld.com/analysis/hondas-ev-u-turn-highlights-the-financial-cost-of-failure) the 0 series is indicative of the difficulty in producing electric vehicles (EVs) profitably, particularly in markets with regulations hostile to their development, such as the US. However, a glimmer of the automaker's former product strategy endures in the 0 Alpha, a small all-electric SUV set to be manufactured in India from 2027.
