As the conflict in Iran continues to destabilise the Middle East, automakers in the luxury segment are treating the situation as one that won't be resolved in the short term. In statements to several publications during the week commencing 16 March, brands like Ferrari, Bentley and Maserati announced that shipments to the region would be paused until further notice.
