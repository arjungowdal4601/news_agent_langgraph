Automotive supplier Marelli will present more than 20 innovations and six new product launches at Auto China 2026 in Beijing, spanning lighting, electronics, interiors, propulsion, green technologies and ride dynamics. The company’s booth at the New China International Exhibition Centre will be organised into six domain-specific areas reflecting that range, with the event running from 24 April to 3 May.

Highlights include a corner-to-corner headlamp and microLED headlamp technologies, an ultra-thin MiniLED display and a next-generation pillar-to-pillar HorizonView display for the cockpit, and an affordable 5G RedCap connectivity solution making its China debut. On the propulsion side, Marelli is unveiling a new e-transmission cooling pump for electric and hybrid vehicles alongside a flex fuel system, while its green technologies portfolio features a high-performance chiller, e-axle oil cooler and full aluminium radiator. The ride dynamics offering covers fully active and hybrid electromechanical suspension systems, active camber and an electromechanical lifter.

![](https://media.automotiveworld.com/app/uploads/2026/03/25111336/marelli-auto-china-2026-beijing-booth.jpg)


The company frames its Beijing presence around a distributed innovation model that combines local engineering and supply chains with global coordination, targeting speed and affordability for customers operating across multiple regions.

In a statement, Joachim Fetzer, Chief Technology and Innovation Officer of Marelli, said: “Our priority is to stand beside our customers where they are, delivering locally impactful innovation, targeted for their needs. This is especially important in China, which is driving automotive evolution in many aspects, but we bring the same approach and commitment wherever customers ask for our support.”
