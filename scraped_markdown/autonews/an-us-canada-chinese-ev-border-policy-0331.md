Chinese cars crossing from Canada into U.S. ‘ain’t gonna happen,’ says Trump envoy

U.S. Ambassador Pete Hoekstra says Chinese EVs imported into Canada won't cross the border, citing security concerns despite Ottawa's new tariff deal with Beijing. (ASSOCIATED PRESS)

A panel of executives at the New York International Auto Show urged more stability in Washington, a strong intracontinental trade deal and localized production of compelling vehicles.
