# What the Coming Ford F-150 EREV Needs to Learn From the F-150 Lightning

Here’s how Ford can perfect the F-150 EREV when it replaces the all-electric Lightning.

![11 2023 Ford F 150 Lightning Lariat on road 6](https://hips.hearstapps.com/mtg-prod/65a1f5d9077e3f0008894e90/11-2023-ford-f-150-lightning-lariat-on-road-6.jpeg?w=768&width=768&q=75&format=webp)

Like many automakers recently, Ford’s future product strategy has been shifting as it tries to keep pace with a rapidly changing market. For Ford, that means pivoting toward more affordable EVs, hybrids, and [ extended-range electric vehicles (EREVs)](https://www.motortrend.com/features/what-is-an-erev-extended-range-ev). As a result, that also meant pivoting away from its all-electric powered

[Ford F-150 Lightning, our 2023 Truck of the Year](https://www.motortrend.com/news/ford-f-150-lightning-2023-truck-of-the-year), which was recently discontinued. In its place, Ford says its next-generation F-150 Lightning will come equipped with an EREV powertrain that will utilize a large battery pack, electric motors, and an internal combustion engine as a power generator.

Although our knowledge of the future EREV-powered Lightning remains limited, we’re *extremely* familiar with the Lightning EV.
