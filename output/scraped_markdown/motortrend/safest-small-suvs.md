# Safest Small SUVs For 2026: IIHS Top Safety Picks and 5-Star NHTSA Ratings

These 10 SUVs met the highest standards from both major U.S. crash-test agencies.

![](https://hips.hearstapps.com/hmg-prod/images/78f94832-fcbf-49f6-a820-20523cb51d6a.jpg?w=768&width=768&q=75&format=webp)

Safety ratings have never mattered more, and the bar has never been higher. For 2026, the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety (IIHS) has again raised the threshold for its Top Safety Pick and Top Safety Pick+ awards, demanding stronger rear passenger protection and more capable pedestrian crash avoidance systems. Meanwhile, the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration’s (NHTSA) 5-star overall rating remains the gold standard for government crash-test credibility.

To build this list, we cross-referenced both agencies and kept only the [small SUVs](https://www.motortrend.com/rankings/suvs/compact) that earned one of the two top honors from the IIHS in addition to scoring a 5-star overall rating from NHTSA, a combination that’s harder to achieve than you might expect. What follows is your guide to the safest small SUVs that your money can buy in 2026.
