Toyota Motor Europe has signed a long-term contract with supply chain software firm Agillence to continue using its Lean Logistics Optimizer (ALLO) platform for inbound parts logistics network planning across Europe. The agreement follows a pilot programme during which ALLO was assessed against Toyota Motor Europe’s logistics network for planning performance, cycle time reduction and carbon neutrality support.

Under the contract, ALLO will provide strategic network planning and scenario analysis to improve efficiency and resilience across Toyota Motor Europe’s inbound logistics operations. The software is designed to handle complex, large-scale automotive logistics networks and will continue in a role it held during the pilot phase.

Jean Christophe Deville, Vice President of Supply Chain, Toyota Motor Europe, said: “ALLO has proven to be a strategic asset in our pursuit of a sustainable, cost-efficient logistics network across Europe. We are confident that this long-term partnership will continue to drive meaningful progress toward our carbon neutrality goals while delivering real operational value.”
