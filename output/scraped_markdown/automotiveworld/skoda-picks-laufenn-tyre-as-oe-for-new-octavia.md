Laufenn’s S Fit 2 summer tyre has been selected as original equipment for the new Škoda Octavia, marking the brand’s debut as a factory fitment for a major European automaker. The tyre will be supplied in 205/55 R17 91 V size for the Czech automaker’s highest-volume model.

Laufenn said the original equipment development focused on producing an all-round performer with strong safety characteristics. The S Fit 2 features a high silica content and convex tread blocks for shorter wet braking distances, while reinforced shoulders and a matched compound are designed to provide stable handling under full load. The tyre has also recently launched in the replacement market.

The supplier said rolling noise was reduced to improve cabin comfort and minimise external noise emissions, while low rolling resistance contributes to fuel efficiency. A new polymer technology is said to increase wear resistance, extending mileage and lowering lifecycle costs for owners.

Laufenn described the Škoda partnership as a foundation for further original equipment projects, underlining what it called the brand’s development over the past decade.
