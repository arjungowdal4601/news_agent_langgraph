Autel Energy has demonstrated its ultra-fast EV charging hardware at Beans2Cup@Ratchada in Bangkok, Thailand, in a live showcase held on 10 April 2026. The event featured the MaxiCharger DS480, DT500 and DT800, which collectively deliver up to 960kW of output through 780A liquid-cooled charging cables.

![](https://media.automotiveworld.com/app/uploads/2026/04/13123141/autel-ev-charging-1024x682.jpg)


During the demonstration, a Zeekr 009 was charged from 20% to 80% in 10 minutes. Compatible performance was recorded across a range of models including the Xpeng X9 and G6, Tesla Model Y, and vehicles from Mercedes-Benz and Porsche. The deployment is supported by local partners B2C Energy, ACharge and Aplus Smart Mobility.

Tanapat Chaijaroenpat, Founder of B2C Energy, said: “Autel’s ultra-fast charging is a key step toward making EV adoption more practical.”

Surachai Pornchindachot, Co-Founder of B2C Energy, added: “By combining high-performance charging with seamless digital experience, Autel is helping build a more accessible EV charging ecosystem in Thailand.”
