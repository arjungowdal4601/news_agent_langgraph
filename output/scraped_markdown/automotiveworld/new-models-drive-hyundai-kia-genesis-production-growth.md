According to *Automotive World's *[latest](https://www.automotiveworld.com/data/global-light-vehicle-production-april-2026-update/) production dataset, the Hyundai Motor Group (HMG) produced slightly more than seven million vehicles last year, and this is expected to reach nearly 7.5 million units in 2026. Asia, specifically Korea and India, accounts for around 70% of production, followed by 15% from North America, 11-12% from Europe, and 3% from South America.
